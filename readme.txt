Give your customers a convenient way to request more information from your sales or support team.

== Description ==

With Synclio Click-To-Call, your customers can connect with you instantly when they need information or assistance. Customers can provide their name and number through the Click-To-Call plugin and you will be notified to reach out to them. Great for support and sales teams, let the leads come to you.

== Installation ==

1. Install the Synclio Click-To-Call Plugin.
2. After activating, you will be prompted to signin or register your Synclio account.
3. After signin or registration, you customers can now use your click to call feature.
4. You can learn more about our <a href="http://www.synclio.com/click-to-call">Synclio Click-To-Call feature here.</a>

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do you have to have Syclio service to run this plugin? =

Yes, but if you don't have it yet don't worry you can join via the plugin install procedure.

= Why isn't this all free? =

When you make a call it has to run through a carrier which charges us money to do so, hence the charge to you.
